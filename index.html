I have a bachelor's degree in computer science from the Subotica Tech - College of Applied Science, Serbia.

I was working as a software developer at a subsidiary of Amatek d.o.o., where they were working on a branch of IoT. The company produced smart motors and their job was to program the motors and create software for them. I also have experience in developing hardware and software tools for smart homes within IoT. My tasks included building a virtual smart home using smart devices.
